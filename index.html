There are many cheap little software programs that claim to spider the web based upon your search criteria. However, to the seasoned marketing veteran, we know better. Scraping software is just that. It’s software. It only does one thing. You enter a keyword and it spiders the internet looking for pages that contain that keyword. Let’s say for example that there is a forum where many people are talking about a particular company. And let’s say they are all very disgruntled about marketers and their approach etc. And let’s say that none of these people were every actively involved with the company. They are merely venting and sharing their frustrations. And let’s say a few of them shared their telephone number in their forum signature. I think you can see where this is going. The software has no way of knowing any of the above. It only does one thing. And that is scrape or harvest the telephone number from the page. And so now let’s say you used software, or you got a list that was scraped, and the list if comprised of numbers that were gathered in this manner. Can you see how this could be a real problem for you? Keep reading to have your eyes opened wide.
